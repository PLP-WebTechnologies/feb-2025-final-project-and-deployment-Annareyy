Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit my recipe to Your Chef's Choice?

We love featuring recipes from our community! Please send your recipe along with high-quality photos to submissions@yourchefschoice.com. Include your name and a brief story about the recipe.

Do you offer cooking classes or workshops?

Yes, we host monthly virtual cooking workshops and occasional in-person classes in Nairobi. Check our Events page or subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated on upcoming classes.

Can I republish your recipes on my website?

Our recipes and photos are copyrighted. You may share a photo with a link back to our website, but please don't republish the full recipe without permission. Contact us for collaboration opportunities.

I have a food product. How can I collaborate with Your Chef's Choice?

We're open to collaborations with brands that align with our values. Please email partnerships@yourchefschoice.com with details about your product and collaboration ideas.
